\section{Problem}
Let F be a sub field of the complex numbers , and let A be the following 2 $\times$ 2 matrix over F.\\
  $\vec{A}=\myvec{2&1\\-1&3}$\\
  
compute f\brak{\vec{A}} for the polynomial
$f\brak{x}=x^{2}-5x+7$
\section{Solution}
Characteristic equation of $\vec{A}$ can be written as:
\begin{align}
    \mydet{\vec{A}-\lambda\vec{I}}=0
\end{align}
\begin{align}
    \myvec{2-\lambda&1\\-1&3-\lambda}=0
\end{align}
 The characteristics equation of the matrix will  be,
 \begin{align}
     \lambda^{2}-5\lambda + 7 = 0
 \end{align}
 The characteristics equation will satisfy its own matrix 
 \begin{align}
     \vec{A}^{2}-5\vec{A}+7\vec{I}=0\label{eq1}
 \end{align}
The given polynomial 
 \begin{align}
     f\brak{x}=x^{2}-5x+7\\
     f\brak{\vec{A}}=\vec{A}^{2}-5\vec{A}+7\vec{I}\label{eq2}
 \end{align}
 substituting \eqref{eq1} in \eqref{eq2} we get
 \begin{align}
     f\brak{\vec{A}}=0
 \end{align}
